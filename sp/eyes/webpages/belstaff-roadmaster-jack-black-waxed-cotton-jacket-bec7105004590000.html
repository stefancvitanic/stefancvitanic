    Belstaff Roadmaster Jack Black Waxed Cotton Jacket

    Description

    A regular fit jacket from Belstaff, constructed from waxed cotton and featuring, a velvet lined stand up collar, with neck buckle fastening, a full dual zip placket, snap fasten storm flap, press stud adjustable cuffs and a straight hem, with an adjustable waist belt. The jacket sports a full tartan patterned lining, twin bellows pockets to the hips, with two pockets to the chest, metal brand badge to the reverse placket, with signature Belstaff branding embroidered to the left sleeve, and subtle Union Jack flag to the chest.
