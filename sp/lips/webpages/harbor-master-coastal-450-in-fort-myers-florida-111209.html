1989 Harbor Master Coastal 450 House in Fort Myers, Florida

Stock #111209

This is a great example of what a houseboat can be. Many of them are pontoon boats with a structure on top. This one is not an ocean going boat but can take a small of chop.

This is a home on the water. Many folks use these for live a board and they are quite comfortable in that capacity.

The main salon is divided into three areas.

In the aft is the "den" with flat screen TV and ample seating.
The middle is the galley, dining and a full head with separate shower.

The forward is the impressive helm. The helm is replete with nautical charm. Plenty of rich wood and a classic "Yacht" wheel.

There is a small berth to the port.

Forward is the captain's stateroom. It features an all in one head and shower.

There is a generous "party" area up top. The outdoor helm is there as well as plenty of seating.

If you are looking for a good live-aboard or a weekend party boat, this could be the one you are looking for.

In leu of a holding tank, this boat is equipped with a Purasan system.

Information On Condition

Despite its age, I found my visual inspection of this craft to be in better than average condition. The interior is in very good condition and it appears the ceiling carpet has been replaced.

This is the expected wear but in my opinion, it is ready for a new owner and not requiring of anything but TLC

Approximate boat location: Fort Myers, Florida
